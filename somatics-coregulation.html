Introduction

I'm **Milandi Mahasin Madiba**, an Indigenous and Black American Master Somaticist & Consultant. My work is for those who are ready to move beyond the limiting norms of a status quo that has caused pain. I am here to help you fully enjoy your life. My approach is rooted in the belief that your body, mind, and emotions are not separate, but a sacred, integrated whole. When we heal the inner conflict caused by this false separation, we can move from stress and pain toward feeling good and living a more joyful, authentic life.
