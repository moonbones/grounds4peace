Introduction

I'm Milandi Mahasin Madiba, Indigenous Black North American Master Somaticist & Consultant. My work is for those who are ready to move beyond the limiting norms of a status quo that has caused pain. I am here to help you fully enjoy your life. My approach is rooted in the belief that your body, mind, and emotions are not separate, but a sacred, integrated whole. When we heal the inner conflict caused by this false separation, we can move from stress and pain toward feeling good and living a more joyful, authentic life.

Approach

In-person sessions, which take place in nature. I facilitate a space where families, couples, and groups can witness, hear, encourage, and support each other. My focus is on helping you deepen your awareness through sensing, feeling, and action—the tools you need to "win" at living your unique life, on your own terms.

Why My Clients Succeed

After 22 years of practice and growing up with a psychologist mother, I believe there is nothing wrong with you. The issues you face are often a response to the circumstances of your life. My role is to help you shift your perspective and improve your experience, not to fix you.

Who I Serve

I am dedicated to serving a diverse range of individuals and communities, and I am an ally to all. My practice is a safe and empowering space for everyone.

  • Participants: Couples, Families, Groups
