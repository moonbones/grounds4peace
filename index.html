Mission:

Grounds for Peace is a transdisciplinary initiative healing the disconnection between people, place, and possibility. We develop programs and catalyze system redesigns across ecological infrastructure, education, agriculture, environmental remediation, and somatics. Our work supports the thriving of both the Earth and the living body‚Äînot as separate, but as co-constitutive grounds of life.

OUR WORK

  1. Somatic Coregulation for Families, Couples & Groups

  2. Living Infrastructure
    We facilitate cross-sector design conversations to replace toxic, rigid urban ground (like concrete sidewalks) with shock-absorbent, water-conductive, tree-root-sensitive surfaces. Our vision is walkable infrastructure that supports biomechanical health, environmental resilience, and rooted beauty.

  3. Regenerative Agriculture
    We advocate for a full transition from nutricidal to nutritious food systems‚Äîrestoring soil, plant, and human vitality while supporting Black, Indigenous, and place-based agricultural wisdom.

  4. Education Without Harm
    We reimagine learning environments by calling for the abolition of physical harm (including forced stillness and chronic sitting) as a basis for education. We support postural freedom, somatic learning, and sensory-responsive environments in schools.

  5. Environmental Remediation & Systems Debriefing
    We convene scientists, Indigenous land stewards, and affected communities to review and refine methods of remediation for toxic soil, air, and water. Our goal is coordinated, culturally accountable, ecologically sound responses to contamination and climate collapse.
